Q. Who is responsible for the Theme Hook Alliance (THA)?
The Theme Hook Alliance be found at https://github.com/zamoose/themehookalliance
The THA is not a component of Opus Primus, the theme only supports the use of
and implementation of the ideas being presented by the THA.
